Hi friends! My name is Melanie

I want to help you to gather your people to create a connected community.
Here are three steps to get you started

  1. Go First
    • Be the one to do the inviting!
    • Most everyone is waiting around to be invited.
    • You do not have to be an extrovert to do this. You can do it!
  2. Get Proximate
    • Start finding your people by being around the people you want in your community
    • Go to the library, hang out in forums, go to the gym
    • People are looking for a friend just like you!
  3. Hold Space
    • Creating a community is about holding space for others, physically and emotionally
    • Holding space means meeting people where they are, as they are
